Service



What is it?

At Marist College, there are numerous opportunities for students to give back to their own community and help those in need.




Benefits

Students will learn the importance of service and selflessness and apply them to their lives.




Extracurriculars under service

Craft group

Students can explore arts and crafts, and be involved in projects. Meetings usually in breaks and/or after school in the fabric room.


Things that would be helpful:

  • Basic knowledge of sewing, using needles etc.




Maybelles

Maybelles is a student service choir at Marist college. Students visit a resthome and perform for the elderly.


No talents or skills required. Just come with a willing, enthusiastic mindset.





Vinnies

A social action group which allows students to learn about those in need and social justice issues in the world through discussion, activities and talks. Students are given opportunities to physically help out in the community alongside Auckland Vinnies (St Vincent de Paul).




Environmental group

A group dedicated to caring for the environment. Students will be given opportunities to learn about environmental issues in the world, and learn how to be more environmentally safe.
