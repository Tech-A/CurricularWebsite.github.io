Music



What is it?

Marist College offers group and individual (seniors only) music lessons taught by highly trained teachers and professional musicians. Students can also join school musical groups and particpate in school concerts or competitions.




Benefits

The benefits of taking a music extracurricular is the skills and knowledge you learn from it.




Extracurriculars under music

Comfortare per Mariam

Comfortare per Mariam is the Marist College choir. It is a combined choir with our brothering school St Paul's. It is open to both juniors and seniors, so if you enjoy singing, go to the open audition (watch out for the notices) or ask one of the music teachers.

Requirements

  • A love for singing :)
  • Audition
  • Clean uniform


Itinerant music

Marist College allows students to learn instruments in private lessons. Paid seperately.

Options

  • Flute
  • Clarinet
  • Saxophone
  • Violin
  • Violincello
  • Guitar
  • Voice
  • Drums

Requirements

  • An instrument of your choice (Some instruments (ie. drums) are able to be borrowed from Marist College in school hours)
